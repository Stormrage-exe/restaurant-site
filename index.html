ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bistro Lumière brings together the rustic charm of Italian trattorias and the refined elegance of Parisian bistros. Chef Marco Laurent, trained in both Florence and Lyon, crafts dishes where handmade pasta meets French techniques.


Our Philosophy

We believe in:
• Seasonal ingredients from local farmers
• Handcrafted pastas and breads daily
• The perfect balance of French sophistication and Italian warmth

The Space

Our dining room features exposed brick walls, vintage chandeliers, and an open kitchen where you can watch our chefs create edible art. The terrace offers panoramic city views at sunset.


Live Jazz Nights

Every Thursday through Saturday, the air fills with the sultry sounds of our house trio:
• 8-10PM: Intimate jazz standards (piano, upright bass, brushed snare)
• 10PM-late: Surprise guest vocalists from the local scene
